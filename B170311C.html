About

The faculty of Engineering and Information Technology (FEIT) is a faculty within the Southern University College (Southern UC). It was established on August 12, 2012 after Southern College (SC) was upgraded to Southern UC. The history reaches back to two decades ago, when the Department of Computer Science was first established in 1991,

followed by the Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering in 2004. These two departments were then integrated and renamed as the School of Engineering and Information Technology (SEIT) in March 2006.
